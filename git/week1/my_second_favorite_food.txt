Description
These creamy, cheesy chicken breasts will, as their title says, melt in your mouth. The recipe is a Yummly original created by Sara Mellas.
Ingredients
US|METRIC
6 SERVINGS
nonstick cooking spray
2 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts (pounded to 3/4-inch thickness, 2 lb. is about 4 breasts)
2 teaspoons salt
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon garlic powder
1/4 cup mayonnaise
1/4 cup plain greek yogurt
1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese

Directions
Preheat the oven to 375Â°F. Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray.
Season the chicken breasts liberally on both sides with the salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Arrange in a single layer in the prepared baking dish.
Stir together the mayonnaise and Greek yogurt in a small bowl. Spread the mixture evenly over the tops of the chicken breasts.
